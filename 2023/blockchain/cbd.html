Call for Paper

2023 The Eleventh International Conference on Advanced Cloud and Big Data (CBD 2023)

December 18-19, 2023, Ocean Flower Island, Hainan, China

Scope of Conference

The International Conference on Advanced Cloud and Big Data (CBD) provides a forum for both academics and practitioners working on cloud computing and big data technologies to explore new ideas, share their experiences and leverage each other's perspectives. Besides the latest research achievements, this conference also covers cloud/big data industry practices and their broad applications, and gives all participants a chance to identify the new emerging “hot” trends in these areas. We solicit original papers on a wide range of cloud computing and big data topics that can be divided into three tracks but are not limited to:

  • Research Track
  • • Cloud Security and Privacy
  • • Cloud Data Center Networking
  • • Cloud Virtualization
  • • Cloud Storage
  • • Multi/Hybrid/Inter Cloud
  • • Big Data Processing/Mining/Query
  • • Big Data Security and Privacy
  • • Edge Computing and its applications
  • • Deep Learning/Federated Learning
  • • Cloud and Big data Management
  • • Blockchain and its Applications
  • • Social Network
  • • Edge Computing Architecture and Algorithm
  • • Machine Learning and AI at Edge
  • Industry Track
  • • Cloud Computing Solutions
  • • Cloud Computing Specifications/Standards
  • • Cloud and Big Data in Industry
  • • Big Data in Smart City/Home
  • • Edge Computing Solutions
  • • Blockchain Solutions
  • Application Track
  • • Cloud based Industrial Internet
  • • Edge-based Industrial Internet
  • • Mobile Edge Computing
  • • Machine/Deep Learning Applications
  • • Social Networking
  • • Knowledge Graph
  • • Blockchain Applications

Paper Submission

Submitted manuscripts must be formatted in standard IEEE US Letter Format and must be submitted via EasyChair as PDF files. The review version is limited to 6 pages (IEEE proceedings format), including references and illustrations. Submitted papers should not be previously published in or be under consideration for publication in another conference or journal. Submission of a paper should be regarded as an undertaking that, should the paper be accepted, at least one of the authors will attend the conference to present the paper.

Publication

All submitted papers will be reviewed by program committee members based on their originality, significance, relevance and clarity of presentation. Accepted papers will be published by Conference Publishing Services (CPS) and will be submitted for indexing to EI (Compendex). Authors of selected papers will be invited to submit revised and expanded version of their papers to be considered for publication in special issues of well-known international journals such as IEEE Network (IF: 10.294), Information Sciences (IF: 8.233), Information Fusion (IF: 17.564), Tsinghua Science & Technology (IF: 3.515), Big Data Mining and Analytics (IEEE), etc.
